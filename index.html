About - This is a mutation of little game 2048. When two tiles with the same icon meet, they merge into one! You may get something unexpected finally according to the theme you selected.

Play - On PC, use the arrow keys ⬆️ , ⬇️ , ⬅️ , ➡️ to move the tiles. On mobile, just swipe your screen.
